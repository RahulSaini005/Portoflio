My Background

I am Rahul Saini, a full-stack developer with a strong command of technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Node.js, and MongoDB. My professional journey is rooted in building practical, scalable, and well-architected solutions that combine clean UI design with efficient backend logic.

I bring a mindset of long-term growth, technical precision, and a deep commitment to building intelligent systems that can scale and evolve. For me, technology is not just about writing code — it’s about engineering the future.
